Maths sites

Here are a few general mathematical web sites

    St Andrews History of Mathematics Archive - biographies of many mathematicians.

    American Mathematical Society links to maths websites - various useful websites.

    The Mathematical Genealogy Project - the academic pedigree of many mathematicians.

    World maths department web sites - links to home pages of maths departments across the world by Penn State University.

    Mathematical and scientific walk around St Andrews - a walk visiting interesting sites in St Andrews.

    Ten Lessons I Wish I Had Been Taught - Gian-Carlo Rota's advice to young mathematicians, from the AMS Notices.

    The Strong Law of Small Numbers - Richard Guy's exposition that 'there aren't enough small numbers to meet the many demands made of them'.

    Let Them Eat Theorems - Informal blog about theorems.
